Signify Health

Through an entrepreneurial and consulting organization called Aggies Create, I had to opportunity to be a software engineering consultant for Signify Health, a company focused on connecting a network of medical providers with patients for at home service, along with a talented team. We were tasked to create an internal data visualization software tool that addressed the issues of visual clutter and a lack of data depth in their current system. Together, we developed Signify Hex, a tool that leverages React/TypeScript, UberH3, Go, PostgreSQL, and other technologies. We used the unique hexagonal shape from UberH3 to solve those problems and show metrics like supply deserts. Here is a short demo.

Project SMART

Since the Spring 2025 semester, I have been an undergraduate research assistant for the project Social Media And Resilient Teens (SMART) lab. This lab aims to investigate the relationship between social media and mental health in adolescents from different ethnic backgrounds in a unique way: by capturing screenshots from teen participants' phones to monitor the content they see on social media. To perform ethical research and protect the participants, I completed a variety of trainings, such as the TrainTraq Child Protection Training. We also went to ILTexas to teach middle school students about internet safety, as shown here. Currently, I am collecting high-quality data by creating surveys in REDCap and conducting participant interviews for the parents and teens in the study.
